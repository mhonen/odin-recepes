Skillet Corn Bread Recipe

Recipe Description

A simple, slightly sweet corn bread made in the

versatile cast-iron skillet.


Ingredients


  • 1 1/4 Cups of milk
  • 1 cup of cornmeal
  • 1 cup of All Purpose flour
  • 4 tespoons of baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon of Kosher salt
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon of Vegetable oil

Steps


  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Place 9 inch cast-iron skillet in oven to warm it.
  2. Mix milk and cornmeal together in a small bowl and let soak for 10 minutes.
  3. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together in a mixing bowl. Beat cornmeal mixture, eggs and butter into the flour mixture until you have a smooth batter, about 1 minute.
  4. Remove skillet from oven. Swish vegetable oil skillet coat; pour off excess.
  5. Pour batter into skillet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cut in wedges to serve.
